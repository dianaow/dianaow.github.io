Passenger Volume by Origin Destination Bus Stops in December 2018

Only routes with more than 10000 trips are included in the chart. There is no specific information of bus service which traveller took on trip from origin to destination bus stops.

Each node represent a bus stop while the arcs represent links from origin to destination bus stops. Arc thickness represent travel volume. Nodes are clustered according to first digit of bus stop code, which also closely follows region bus stop is located in. Nodes are then sorted according to bus stop codes in ascending order.

Data Source: LTA
