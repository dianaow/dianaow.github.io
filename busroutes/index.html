Singapore's Bus Routes

All bus stops and the routes that pass through them are visualized on the map. Red lines represent routes in 'Direction 1', while blue lines represent routes in 'Direction 2'

Select a bus service number to visualize its route. This will clear out the map and only show one route.

Note: I do not have the exact coordinates of path taken by each route. The smoothness of a route is dependent on the distance between bus stops. This explains the presence of straight lines that unrealistically cut across land in some routes. Data Source: LTA
