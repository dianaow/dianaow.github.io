Data Visualization Society members in 2020

PixiJS

The Data Visualization Society released their 2020 survey results and I participated in visualizing the results, choosing to represent all respondents as animating and interactive dots on the screen. I wanted audiences to be able to find out how represented they are in the data viz community based on their skillset.

I allowed audiences to explore by funneling the technology/tool selection with dropdowns, hoping to create a unique data experience with the particle animation.
