Specializing in Data Visualization

I choose to focus on data visualization as i believe its the best way to get people interested in taking an analytical view into a dataset. It incites excitement by creating a compelling story for the dataset. To me, each dataset has its own quirks and the task of a data visualizer is to create a medium to present the results in the most engaging and and appropriate manner. I enjoy using Vega-lite to create classic line and bar charts and exploiting D3's layout and transition functions for custom designs. In particular, I am focused on building expertise in geovisualization with tools such as Leaflet and Mapbox JS GL.
